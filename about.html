Danish Mehboob is a senior reporter with Euromoney Institutional Investor. He is also an alum of CNNMoney and CNNMoneyStream. He writes about finance and markets, but other interests include immigration, politics, and religion.

Mehboob has an MS degree from Columbia University combining data science and investigative journalism. His skills include social media verification, analytics, design, and code. He spends time mining through datasets along with building websites and graphics for story packages. Mehboob’s work has appeared in CNN, News Deeply, UPI and many other publications. He’s on the lookout for full time positions in news and media.
